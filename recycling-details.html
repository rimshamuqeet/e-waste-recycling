Prepare Your Device for Recycling

Learn how to back up your data and wipe your device clean.

How to Prepare Your Device

  • Back Up Your Data:
    • Connect your device to a computer or cloud service.
    • Transfer important files, photos, and contacts to a secure location.
    • Use built-in backup tools (e.g., iCloud for iOS, Google Drive for Android).
  • Sign Out of Accounts:
    • Sign out of all accounts (e.g., Google, Apple, social media).
    • Remove any linked payment methods.
  • Factory Reset Your Device:
    • Go to your device's settings and find the "Reset" option.
    • Select "Erase All Data" or "Factory Reset."
    • Confirm the action to wipe your device clean.
  • Remove SIM and SD Cards:
    • Remove any SIM or SD cards from your device.
    • Keep these for future use or dispose of them securely.

Why Prepare Your Device?

Properly preparing your device ensures that your personal data is secure and that the device can be recycled efficiently. It also helps protect the environment by ensuring that hazardous materials are handled correctly.
