What is E-Waste?

E-waste refers to discarded electronic devices like phones, laptops, and TVs. These devices contain harmful chemicals that can pollute the environment if not disposed of properly.

Why Recycle E-Waste?

Recycling e-waste helps recover valuable materials like gold, silver, and copper. It also reduces pollution and protects our planet.
